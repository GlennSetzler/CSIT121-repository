Chili


Creator: Glenn Setzler

Ingredients
  • 1 Can of whole corn (drained)
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Green pepper
  • 1 Stalk of celery with leaves
  • 2 Jalapeno peppers
  • 12oz. Hot cilli sauce
  • 12oz. Cans tomato paste
  • 12oz. Cans tomato sauce
  • Hot ssauce to taste
  • 4 15oz. Cans black beans
  • 1 Can beer
  • 1 14 1/2 oz. Cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 14 1/2 oz. Cans diced tomatoes with basil and garlic (can substitute Italian)
  • 1 T. Cumin
  • 1 T. Chili powder
  • 1 T. Cruch red peppers
  • 1 lb. Ground beef
  • 1 lb. Ground deer or lamb
  • 1 lb. Cubed beef or lamb
  • 1 Package of beef sausage

Steps for prep
  1. Dice half of vegetables
  2. Put other half in a blender on mince till you have a chunky paste
  3. Fry ground beef, deer and/or lamb in a skillet until half done
  4. Add chunky vegetable paste to skillet with ground meat until meat is done
  5. Put all ingredients (including the finished ground meat and vegetable paste) exept the beans into a large pot and let simmer for 2 hours, stirring occasionally
  6. Taste occasionally and add more seasonings, or tomato sauce/paste as needed
  7. While chili is cooking drain and wash all beans untill the chili is done than add the beans
